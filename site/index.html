Issue No. 1 | March 2023

Dear Colleagues,

I am excited to introduce our inaugural tech newsletter, brought to you by SPH Tech. This monthly newsletter serves as a platform to keep everyone in the organisation up-to-date on the activities within Tech Group. Our goal is to demystify the work that we do and enhance collaboration between departments by fostering open and transparent communications.

In these newsletters, we will be sharing updates on the projects we're working on, highlighting new technologies and tools, and providing insights into the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead. We believe that by fostering a better understanding of the role technology plays in our organisation, we can work together to drive innovation and success.

I encourage you to read the newsletter and reach out to us with any questions or feedback. We're always looking for ways to improve and create value and impact for our organisation.

Sincerely,

Kaythaya Maw
C.T.O
