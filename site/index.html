Dear Colleagues,

I am excited to introduce our inaugural tech newsletter, brought to you by SPH Tech. This monthly newsletter serves as a platform to keep everyone in the organisation up-to-date on the activities within Tech Group. Our goal is to demystify the work that we do and enhance collaboration between departments by fostering open and transparent communications.

In these newsletters, we will be sharing updates on our key projects and initiatives, highlighting new technologies and tools, and providing insights on how digital can improve the way we work, operate and collaborate. We believe that by fostering a better understanding of the expanding role technology plays in our organisation, we can work together to drive innovation and success. As a live example of rapidly expanding possibilities enabled by tech, most images in this newsletter issue are created through Generative AI technology using the DALL-E tool.

I encourage you to read the newsletter and reach out to us with any questions or feedback. We're always looking for ways to improve and create value and impact for our organisation.

Sincerely,

Kaythaya Maw
CTO
